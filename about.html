About EaziEats

EaziEats is a for-profit online food ordering business created to provide customers with convenient access to delicious meals, snacks, and drinks through a simple and modern website.

Slogan

Fast. Fresh. Delivered the Easy Way.

Mission

To provide fast, affordable, and satisfying meals through a simple and reliable online shopping experience.

Vision

To become one of Jamaica's most trusted online food ordering brands through convenience, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Target Audience

University students, busy office workers, and families seeking quick and convenient meals.
